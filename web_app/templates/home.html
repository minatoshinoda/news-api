Welcome to "What's The Buzz?"

This site was created to boost student engagement with current news by efficiently providing them a source of convenient, trustworthy, and priority news content. All the data is pulled using the News API, which constantly gathers the most recent articles across news sites and blogs across the US.

To generate articles, please refer to the three bar menu on the top right corner and select the article generator. Once there, you will be prompted to select a topic of interest from a drop-down menu. This will generate the five most recent articles or blogs posted under that topic, with a short description of their content below, for those looking to absorb information quickly.
